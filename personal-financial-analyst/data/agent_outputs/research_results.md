### 1. Netflix - $15.99/month (Streaming)

**Current Cost:** $15.99/month

#### Alternatives

**Option A: Netflix Ad-Supported Tier**
- **Cost:** $6.99/month
- **Monthly Savings:** $9.00

- **Cons:** Ads interrupt viewing experience; some content limited to ad-free tier; lower video quality (720p vs 1080p+)
- **Recommendation:** Consider if ad tolerance is high

**Option B: Disney+ Basic**
- **Cost:** $7.99/month
- **Monthly Savings:** $8.00 (as replacement)
- **Pros:** Extensive Disney/Marvel/Star Wars catalog; growing original content
- **Cons:** Different content library; less diverse content vs Netflix

**Option C: Max (HBO Max) Ad-Supported**
- **Cost:** $9.99/month
- **Monthly Savings:** $6.00
- **Pros:** Quality HBO originals; broader content library
- **Recommendation:** Bundle with Amazon Prime for maximum value

**Best Recommendation:** Switch to Netflix Ad-Supported tier and save **$9.00/month ($108/year)**
